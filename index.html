Our Story

"Baking is about creating memories and connecting hearts across mountains and oceans."

Claudia

I grew up in a small village in the West of Austria, surrounded by mountains, spruce & fir, ash, beech & hazel trees. Gurgling of the Rhine river in my ears. My Mama's hands sewing summer dresses for her daughters or kneading dough. The kitchen hot & sweet, drawing everyone closer.

Moving to Colorado as a young Mom, my heart yearned for all those I've left behind and I began to bake Austrian pastries week after week, year after year, charming new friends. Slowly I was known by this land, tended to for a long time by many indigenous peoples. As an herbalist I fell in love with ponderosa pine, chokecherry & gumweed and many plants I was already familiar with. My own roots began to grow deeper & wider and I try to move gently.

Dominik

Growing up in Colorado with Austrian roots has given me a unique perspective on baking and culture. From an early age, I watched my mother create magical treats that transported us back to her homeland. These experiences inspired my dream of opening this bakery.

After studying culinary arts and apprenticing in various bakeries across Austria and Colorado, I've developed a style that honors both my heritage and my home. Working alongside my mother in our bakery is the realization of a lifelong dream, allowing us to share our family's traditions with our community.
