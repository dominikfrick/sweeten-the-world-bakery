Our Story

Bringing together the rich baking traditions of the Austrian Alps and the spirit of the Rocky Mountains, Sweeten the World Bakery creates unique, handcrafted pastries that tell a story of two mountain cultures.

Our Specialties

Poppy Moon Claw (MohnKrone)

A delicate, crescent-shaped pastry filled with rich poppy seed paste, offering a subtle hint of sweetness and a touch of nostalgia for classic Austrian flavors.

Cinnamon Apple Roll (Zimtschnecke)

Soft and tender dough filled with cinnamon and fresh apple, offering a balanced sweetness and warmth in every bite.

Walnut Horn (Nuskipferl)

A sweet, flaky pastry with a rich walnut filling, inspired by Austrian baking traditions. Perfect with a cup of coffee or tea.

Rose Knot (Rosen Knopf)

Elegant, braided dough with a hint of rosewater, crafted to resemble a blooming rose and add a floral sweetness to your day.

Mini Marble Cakes (Gugelhupf)

Our traditional marble cakes in miniature form, offering layers of vanilla and chocolate in every bite-size serving.
